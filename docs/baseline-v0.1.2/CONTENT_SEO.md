# CONTENT_SEO.md — Blog, Guides and Glossary

Дата: 2026-07-16
Статус: **accepted product requirement**

## Роль

Блог WebDiag — отдельный SEO, education и acquisition layer. Он не входит в число 110 инструментов.

## Разделы

- `/blog/` и `/en/blog/`;
- `/guides/` и `/en/guides/`;
- `/glossary/` и `/en/glossary/`.

## Стартовые кластеры

1. Technical SEO.
2. Robots.txt, sitemap.xml, canonical, hreflang и indexability.
3. Redirects, broken links и crawlability.
4. Core Web Vitals и performance.
5. SSL, HTTP headers и web security basics.
6. Uptime, incidents и monitoring.
7. SEO changes и regression detection.
8. Structured data.
9. Image SEO, formats, compression, crop и resize.
10. Developer tools и web standards.
11. WebDiag methodology и product updates.

## Обязательные поля

- content id;
- locale;
- translation group;
- title, slug, description, H1;
- author and reviewer;
- source references;
- created, updated, last reviewed, review due;
- applicable product/check/standard version;
- related tools, findings and guides;
- canonical, indexation and editorial status.

## Publication gates

Запрещены thin pages, массовый AI filler, doorway content, недоказанные claims и непроверенный машинный перевод.

Основной путь конверсии:

`Article → relevant tool → finding explanation → audit/monitoring`
